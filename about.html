We are Gratis Boligsalg

Gratis Boligsalg is an alternative to the traditional sale of housing by offering a digitized self-service platform as a link between the seller and the buyer, there by cutting away the real estateagent as an unnecessarily expensive intermediary.
Sellers often find it difficult to understand what they are actually paying a real estate agent for. Real estate agents find it difficult to differentiate their products/services, which is why it often becomes a gut feeling that becomes crucial to the choice of a real estate agent. Common to all sellers’ is the perception of the price, which is too high.
With Gratis Boligsalg, the seller himself acts as a real estate agent and can place the fee directly in his own pocket.
